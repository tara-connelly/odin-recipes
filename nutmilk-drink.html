Nutmilk Drink

Description:

This nutmilk drink is not only nourishing, but gives you energy for more hours than your traditional breakfast and is vegan!

Note: this recipe makes 6 full large glasses.

Ingredients

This list is for 6 servings
  • 1 stick cinnamon (not the fake stuff)
  • 15 cardomom pods with shell removed
  • 6 cloves
  • 200 mg brazil nuts
  • 200 mg almonds
  • 200 mg pecans
  • 300 mg pure malt extract

This list is for your daily 2 servings

  • 2-3 ripe bananas
  • 2 pitted dates
  • 10 slightly toasted cocoa beans (without shell)

Preparation Steps

  1. Blend the cardamom, cinnamon, and clove in a high-powered motor blender, until a fine spice powder.
  2. Add all the nuts into the spice powder, blend until blender for about 15 seconds, when it no longer can blend well.
  3. Add water to nutmilk blend, and blend for a few seconds.
  4. Repeat last step until a fine, thick nutmilk blend with water
  5. Fill large mason jar with malt extract.
  6. Add nutmilk blend to mason jar with malt.
  7. Add distilled or very purified water to nutmilk and malt blend and shake until malt is blended.
    8. Continue to add water until reach top of jar.

Daily Use Steps

  1. Measure oh 800 mg of nutmilk in blender cup
  2. Add 2-3 ripe bananas and dates into nutmilk cup
  3. Blend until smooth
  4. Chill until ready to serve.
